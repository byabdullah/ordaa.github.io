History

Ethiopia

Ethiopia is the oldest independent country in Africa and the world. The country that comprises of more than 80 ethnic groups is the second most populated country next to Nigeria with more than 100 million populations. Unique among African countries, Ethiopia has never been colonized, one exception occupied by Italy in 1936-41.

In history of Abrahamic religion of the world, Ethiopia that has 45 times quoted in the Bible was the first place where the first journey (Hijra) in the history of Islam took place. Addis Ababa, the capital of the country, is also known as the "capital and diplomatic hub of Africa" is also the center of many African-oriented international organizations such as the African Union, the Pan-African Trade Chamber. Even if Ethiopia is the country with the rapidly growing economy in Africa today, large part of its people live in hazardous conditions due to internal and external problems. Presently displaced people (more of children and women), illiteracy, drought and Famine are common features of the country. Hence, to reverse impoverished condition of Ethiopians based on research and development method, establishing ORDA is of great importance.

ODA RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION

ORDA is an acronym of Oda Research and Development Association. Oda has meaningful value and respect in Ethiopia as is Çınar in Turkey. Oda and Çınar trees symbolize an important socio-political value in Ethiopia and Turkey respectively. The longevity and durability of these trees ties the past into the future, teaches people patience in life, wisdom of co-existence and being useful to each other.

ORDA primarily focuses on building human capital of Ethiopian students in Turkey. ORDA then directly undertakes projects and programs, which can bring changes that could have long-term effects in improving the lives of the needy communities in Ethiopia. ORDA is also aimed to play an ambassadorial role between Turkey and Ethiopia.

Detailed Services

Ethiopia and Turkey Networking

  • Facilitating the efforts of strengthening partnerships between Ethiopian and Turkey in all aspects of developments.
  • Supporting and promoting indigenous excellence in partnership with academic institutions and other stakeholders.

Water

  • One person out of ten cannot reach clean water for drink in this world. It gets worse when Ethiopia is the case. Drought, rapid population growth, and pollution threaten the water resources. In addition, poverty and lack of now how for getting clean water created more humanitarian crises in Ethiopia.
  • A Donor can commission a water well of regular standards for the fixed price of 4.500 USD. Apart from standard ones, we give quotes for wells especially specialized wells depending on the project.

Technology Transfer

The main reason for the poverty in Ethiopia is not the scarcity of resource. However, it is due to mismanagement of resources, lack of skills and know-how of how to use the resource as well as unfair control of vast majority of the resource in the hands of few. To solve these problems the focuses of ORDA will be on building human capital that can transform their country by using all the useful skills and expertise they develop during their stay in Turkey that are available in Turkey. ORDA plans to directly undertakes projects and programs which bring changes that could have long term effects in improving the lives of the needy communities in Ethiopia.

Orphans

In Ethiopia due to social and political crises, there are many children who (insert who here) lost their parents and cannot afford means of living.

If you would like to sponsor individual orphans, support orphanage projects or building an orphanage please contact us.

Health Care and Education

  • People in Ethiopia are striving under difficult circumstances due to unavailability for sheltering and health, which are among the basic needs of human being. Recently many peoples are left houseless due to internal displacement following social and political instability in the country. Healthcare centers and hospitals constructed in regions suffering from war or poverty can be lifesaving.
  • The inadequacy of financial resources, lack of school buildings and physical conditions, lack of teachers, and teaching material have a negative impact on the lives and future of people in poverty-stricken countries such as Ethiopia.

Emergency Relief

Millions of people in Ethiopia are victims of wars, conflicts, natural disasters, epidemics, diseases, poverty and drought. While we intervene in emergencies, we aim to bring service to the regions in Ethiopia in terms of healthcare, food, shelter and education.

General Objectives

Give a man a fish, and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish, and you feed him for a lifetime. Teach a man how to make fishing instrument of you will make him independent. (This Ancient Chinese proverb is the basis of ORDA’s guiding principle).

Building the capacity of Ethiopians to transfer useful social values, skills and technology from Turkey to Ethiopia.

Serving as a bridge between different governmental and non-governmental organizations of Turkey and Ethiopia on development issues.

Undertaking researches and projects that can improve the economy, education and other social conditions of the country.

To provide humanitarian aids on areas of clean drinking water, orphan support, health, education, and emergency relief for the needy community in the countrytechnical and material supports for health centers and schools in Ethiopia.

Specific Objectives

Identifying barriers to active participation of youths, girls and women in development works and adapting practices that could increase their participation and self-realization.

Undertaking researches and projects on transferable practices to develop societal values and work culture.

Participating stakeholders to cooperate in the realization of improved living standards of the needy communities.

Working on projects that can create job opportunities for the needy communities.

Supplying clean water to the poor communities.

Developing platforms that could enable participation of different governmental and non-governmental development partners.

Organizing capacity building trainings and experience sharing programs to enhance skill transfer from Turkey to Ethiopia.

Engaging in relief and rehabilitation aids during natural and man-made disasters

Supporting orphan children and orphanages.

Facilitating technical and material supports for health centers and schools in Ethiopia.

Vision

ORDA aspires to see competent human capital who can play active roles in creating a resilient society that can solve its own problems and challenges.


Mission

ORDA’s mission is empowering Ethiopians through various research, transferable skills and capacity building programs to build better human capital and economy.
